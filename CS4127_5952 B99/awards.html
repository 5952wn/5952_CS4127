Awards and Nominations

Brooklyn Nine-Nine has received numerous awards and nominations for its innovative storytelling, diverse represntation, and comdeic brilliance. Here are some highlights:

Emmy Awards

  • Best Comedy Series - Nominated in 2014, 2015
  • Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series Andre Braugher (Captain Holt) (2014, 2015, 2016)
  • Outstanding Original Music and Lyrics- "One Last Ride" (2019)

Golden Globe Awards

  • Best Television Series- Musical or Comedy- Nominated in 2014
  • Best Actor in a Television Series- Musical or Comedy- Andy Samberg (Detective Jake Peralta) Nominated in 2015

Critics' Choice Television Awards

  • Best Comedy Series- Nominated in 2014
  • Best Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series- Andre Braugher (Nominated)

People's Choice Awards

  • Favorite Network Comedy- Winner in 2015
  • Favorite Comedic TV Actor- Andy Samberg (Detective Jake Peralta)(2015)

GLAAD Media Awards

  • Outstanding Comedy Series- Nominated in 2014, 2015
  • Outstanding Individual Episode- "Terry Kitties" (2016)
